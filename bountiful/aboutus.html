A Brief History

We began this effort many years ago after hearing about our friends' struggles getting their kids to eat something, anything! with nutritional value. There must be, we reasoned, a call for healthy alternatives to the high-sugar, processed snacks found ubiquitously. After some trials with our own children, we found that there was indeed a marked for healthy drinks, made fresh from the delicious fruit so abundantly available in our region.

After all these years, we continue to take delight in the flavor combinations available through our made-to-order fresh smoothies. Our fleet of smoothie trucks is ready to introduce the next generation of health-conscious families to the joys of natural nutrition. Whether you are visiting the area or looking for refreshment after an afternoon at the gym or beach, we have a fresh smoothie for you!
