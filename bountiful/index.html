Torrey Pines

In addition to being the only place on earth where the Torrey Pine will grow, this beautiful area has a storied history and a network of hiking trails that offers spectacular views of the Pacific Ocean. You may also see paragliders riding the ridge from the nearby flight park. Beyond the spectacular Torrey Pine, you will see magnificent wildflowers while taking in the sea air.
