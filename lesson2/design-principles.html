Repetition

As with many news sites, ksl.com uses repetition of elements to create a harmonious collection of stories. Each story shows with the same size and position of photo and headline, which helps convey that each is part of the whole.

Contrast

This example demonstrates the use of contrast to create impact. In addition to making the site more readable, the contrast helps the white color of the title stand out against the background image of each section. The contrast of the background against the white background also clearly separates each section.

Alignment

The instructure interface works well on mobile devices. In this view of the modules page, assignments for each week align, providing a logical grouping. The week section headers also align, conveying a sense of hierarchy between weeks and their contents.
