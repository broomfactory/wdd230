Repetition

As with many news sites, ksl.com uses repetition of elements to create a harmonious collection of stories. Each story in the listing appears with a square thumbnail image and the headline. The repetition of these elements helps convey the idea that the site is a collection of stories: a repository of current events.

Contrast

This example from the Adafruit website demonstrates the use of contrast to create impact. In addition to making the site more readable, the contrast helps the white color of the title stand out against the background image of each section. The darker colors of each image contrast nicely with the white background, creating a clear separation between sections.

Alignment

The instructure interface works well on mobile devices. In this view of the modules page, assignments for each week align, providing a logical grouping. The week section headers also align, conveying a sense of hierarchy between weeks and their contents. Consistent alignment also creates a visually pleasing appearance. The alignment of elements on canvas naturally draws the eye along, suggesting that the page contents continue downward.
