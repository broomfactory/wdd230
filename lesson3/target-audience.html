Target Audience

Personas

Business Owner

Someone who runs a business in the city. They tend to represent diverse backgrounds in culture and gender. These constitute the primary group of supporting chamber members. They are concerned with enacting policies that will help city businesses to thrive and grow.

Policy Maker

This persona consists of members of city government, councils, law-enforcement, code-enforcement, and public liasons of the same. They engage with other chamber members to help guide policy and legislation to encourage investment in the city by business owners.

Scenarios

  • What is the city doing about homelessness? How do they plan to meet the needs of the unsheltered? What policies exist to reduce the negative impact of crime in the area?
  • What development and revitalization plans have been proposed? Has funding been secured?
  • How can I be more involved in policy that affects my business?
  • As a policy-maker, what do I need to know about the desires of business owners in the area?
  • How can we create an environment where business owners, customers, and visitors feel safe and are excited to explore the city?
